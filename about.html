About Me

Home / About me

Simple but not simple minded. Our motive is to help you discover ways to feel more connected to your home and work place. To enjoy a sense of completeness. Our company provides integrated solutions that not only make your offices and homes look great but also create an optimal environment for achieving your corporate and personal lifestyle goals.

Amitabh Talreja

Founder and principal designer

The consultancy specializes in interior design & project management of its interior design. The range of project includes corporate offices, showroom, residences, bungalows & hospitals. The emphasis in our work has been on innovative planning to maximize space utilization & development of properly integrated systems & services into an aesthetically designed concept. The consultancy has sufficient exposure to internationally adopted systems & standards. Founded by principal designer Amitabh Talreja. KL Talreja Associates started the office in 2001 in Mumbai. Since its inception, KL Talreja associates has designed & built many outstanding spaces that reflect a unique design vision & craft. Under the direction of principal designer, the office strives to create powerful & clear design solutions individual to each project & to each client. The office is composed of experienced, talented & creative professionals capable of meeting all the demand of the project. The firm operates from a uniquely designed studio with the infrastructure to handle projects all over India & abroad. Setting K L Talreja apart is our commitment to total customer satisfaction and strict adherence to completion & delivery schedules. No matter what the space, no matter what the need, K L Talreja will define form to create business and working environments that are expressions of excellence. We manage the project for you from the initial space planning through product selections to hanging the last piece of artwork on your walls. We give you not only value but imagination. K L Talreja associates thrives on collaborative work with clients. The firms’ primary interest is to create a unique design of its own. Our team of designers strives to create powerful & clear design. Individual to each client & to each project. Success is a result of firms’ dedication to ongoing experiment with Design Material & method of production.
